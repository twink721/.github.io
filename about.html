Hello. My name is Twinkle and I am a rising senior. I created this website as my Activist Toolkit project for the Girls Who Code SIP Program. I got interested in Women's Health and Menstruation last summer. Periods are such a taboo topic, I personally also struggled to normalize this topic for many years. In my junior year, my friends and I started a PERIOD chapter at our school. Period poverty is more severe than we think, I hope this inspires others to delve into the issues and create more change!
